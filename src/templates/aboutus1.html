    QR Code Generation

    We specialize in generating QR codes for each tablet sheet with fields including:

    • Product ID: Check the status by clicking the "Check Status" button.
    • Product Name: Check the status by clicking the "Check Status" button.
    • Manufacturer Information
    • Lot or Batch Number: Must be unique. Check the status by clicking the "Check Status" button.
    • Manufacturing Date
    • Expiration Date
    • Serial Number: Check the status by clicking the "Check Status" button.
    • Unique Device Identifier (UDI)

    Blockchain Technology Solution

    Due to security concerns with the use of isopropyl and other chemicals leading to misinformation, we utilize blockchain technology to generate hash values and QR codes. Our solution aims to:

    • Enhance transparency and safety in the pharmaceutical industry.
    • Implement comprehensive packaging standards providing crucial information to consumers.
    • Empower consumers with accurate and up-to-date information about the products they consume.
    • Significantly reduce the death rate by 85% through improved visibility of manufacturing details.
